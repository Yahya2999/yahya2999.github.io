Privacy Policy

Zikr & Dua · Updated: June 2026

Summary: Zikr & Dua does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All counter data is stored only on your device.

1. Data we collect

We do not collect any personal data — no name, phone number, email, or location.

2. Local storage

The app stores your dhikr counter, selected language, and sound setting only on your device. This data is never sent to any server and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Internet access

The app connects to the internet only when you tap the Telegram banner to open an external Quran-learning channel. No data is shared during this.

4. Permissions

The app may use device vibration for haptic feedback while counting. This permission does not grant access to any personal data.

5. Ads & analytics

The app contains no advertising and uses no third-party analytics or ad SDKs.

6. Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no personal data, posing no privacy risk to children.

7. Purpose

Zikr & Dua is a free, non-commercial project (sadaqah jariyah) created to help Muslims with dhikr and dua. We do not sell data or monetize users.
